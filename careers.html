CAREERS

We hire when we
need someone, not on a schedule.

We're a small team. There's no list of open roles — when there's real work that needs someone to own it, we look for that person.

What We Look For

You've shipped something.

We care more about what you've built than what you can describe in an interview.

You've lived this problem.

Grading, teaching, learning, course-building — if you've actually done it, you'll build better software for it than someone who's only studied it.

You're okay without a finished spec.

Labs and Learn are still in beta. You'd be helping define them, not just building to a doc.

You think past the next release.

We'd rather ship something slower and have it still work next year.
